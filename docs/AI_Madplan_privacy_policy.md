# Privacy Policy for AI Madplan

**Last updated:** January 5, 2026

## Introduction

AI Madplan ("we", "our", or "the app") is a meal planning application that uses artificial intelligence to generate vegetarian meal plans. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Data You Provide
- **Meal preferences**: Number of people, dietary restrictions, blacklisted ingredients
- **API keys**: Optional API keys for AI services (Google Gemini, OpenRouter)
- **Firebase configuration**: Optional cloud sync credentials you provide

### Data Generated by the App
- **Meal plans**: AI-generated recipes and shopping lists
- **Recipe history**: Previously used recipes for variety tracking
- **Shopping list status**: Checked/unchecked items

### Data We Do NOT Collect
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
- Location data
- Device identifiers for tracking
- Usage analytics or telemetry

## How Your Data is Stored

### Local Storage
All app data is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage:
- Meal plans and preferences are stored in local Hive databases
- API keys are stored in secure encrypted storage (flutter_secure_storage)
- No data is sent to our servers

### Optional Cloud Sync (Firebase)
If you choose to enable cloud sync:
- You provide your own Firebase project credentials
- Data is stored in your personal Firebase Firestore database
- We have no access to your Firebase project or data
- You control your data entirely through your Firebase console

## Third-Party Services

### AI Providers
When generating meal plans, your preferences are sent to your chosen AI provider:

**Google Gemini**
- Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- Only meal preferences are sent (no personal data)

**OpenRouter**
- Privacy Policy: https://openrouter.ai/privacy
- Only meal preferences are sent (no personal data)

### Firebase (Optional)
If you enable cloud sync using your own Firebase project:
- Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
- You are the data controller for your Firebase project

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties. Your data is:
- Stored locally on your device, or
- Stored in your personal Firebase project (if you enable sync)

## Your Rights and Controls

You have full control over your data:
- **View**: All data is visible within the app
- **Export**: Export all data as JSON from Settings
- **Delete**: Clear all data from Settings or uninstall the app
- **Cloud sync**: Disconnect and delete cloud data anytime

## Data Security

We implement security measures including:
- Encrypted local storage for sensitive data (API keys)
- No transmission of data to our servers
- Optional end-to-end control via your own Firebase project

## Children's Privacy

AI Madplan does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is intended for general audiences planning household meals.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
