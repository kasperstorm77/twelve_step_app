# Family Sharing — Privacy Policy

*Last updated: 2026-04-17*

Family Sharing is a recipe-sharing app. The app reads and writes recipes
and images **inside the user's own Google Drive**, in a folder named
"Family Sharing" which the user creates or is given access to.

The app's developer (Kasper Storm) does not operate a backend. No user
data is transmitted to, stored on, or accessible by the developer's servers.
All data stays in the user's own Google Drive and under the user's sole
control.

The app uses Google Sign-In to authenticate. Google handles all
authentication; the app receives only the user's Google account email and
display name, and only for display within the app itself. The email is not
exported, logged, or shared.

The app requests access to Google Drive for the purpose of storing and
syncing recipe data with family members who have been granted access to
the same shared folder. It does not access any Drive content unrelated to
the Family Sharing folder.
